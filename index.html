Meiosis

A website that demonstrates meiosis

Start Chromosomes:

Interphase: Parent cell contains __ chromosomes and is haploid

Prophase: Parent chromosomes grow duplicates, resulting in a diploid cell. chromosomes "cross over" (mix genes). This results in __ chromosomes

Prometaphase: Nucleus dissolves and Microtubules attatch to chromosomes.

Metaphase 1: Next, chromosomes align at middle of the cell.

Anaphase 1: Chromosomes pull apart.

Telophase 1: Microtubules dissolve.

Interphase 2: Two cells form with __ chromosomes

Metaphase 2: Microtubules attach to centromeres and chromosomes.

Anaphase 2: Chromosomes are pulled apart.

Telophase 2: cell division beghins.

Cytokinesis: 4 cells are formed, each with different DNA. These are sex cells. Each cell contains __ chromosomes.
